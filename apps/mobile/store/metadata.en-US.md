# SHADOWGRID

Power is invisible. Traces are not.

Build a fictional organization across the Vesper Metropolitan Zone. Manage legitimate businesses, specialists, information, diplomacy and long-term stability in a server-authoritative seasonal strategy game. All places, people and covert actions are fictional and abstract.

Launch features include eight districts, five business types, facilities, eight specialist roles, qualitative-risk operations, organizations, treaties, research, news and seasonal rankings.
